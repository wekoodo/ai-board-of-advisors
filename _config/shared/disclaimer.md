## No Professional Relationship

Using this system does not create:
- An attorney-client relationship with any advisor
- A CPA-client or tax advisory relationship
- A fiduciary or investment advisory relationship regulated by the SEC or FINRA
- Any other licensed professional relationship

## Consult Licensed Professionals

Before implementing any recommendation, strategy, or plan discussed in a Board of Advisors
session, you must consult with a qualified, licensed professional in the relevant jurisdiction:

- **Financial planning:** Certified Financial Planner (CFP) or Registered Investment Advisor (RIA)
- **Tax matters:** Certified Public Accountant (CPA) or Enrolled Agent (EA)
- **Legal matters:** Attorney licensed in your state/jurisdiction
- **Investment decisions:** Registered Investment Advisor (RIA) or licensed broker-dealer
- **Real estate transactions:** Licensed real estate broker or agent in your jurisdiction
- **Estate planning:** Attorney specializing in estate law in your jurisdiction
- **Insurance:** Licensed insurance agent or broker in your state
