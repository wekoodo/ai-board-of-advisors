## When to Load

Load `frameworks.md` when:
- You are assessing whether a market or site supports a self-storage facility
- The conversation involves underwriting a ground-up development (lease-up, yield-on-cost)
- The client wants to optimize operations, occupancy, rate, or expenses on an existing facility
- You are running due diligence on a potential acquisition

**Do not load unless directly applicable.** Keep the context window lean.
