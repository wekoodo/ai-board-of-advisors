## When to Load

Load `frameworks.md` when:
- Client needs a bracket analysis or effective rate calculation
- Entity type comparison is needed (tax treatment differences)
- Estimated quarterly tax payments need to be calculated
- Deduction strategy is a focus (above/below the line)
- S-Corp election or payroll optimization is relevant
- A Roth conversion is being evaluated

**Do not load unless directly applicable.** Keep the context window lean.
